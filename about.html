WHO WE ARE

We're a venture software house that partners with domain experts to build transformative tech companies. We provide the technical co-founder that great ideas deserve.

OUR STORY

Born from Frustration

Awasero started when our founders kept meeting the same person: brilliant industry experts with transformative ideas who couldn't find technical partners willing to bet on their vision.

These weren't people lacking drive or intelligence. They were executives, practitioners, and entrepreneurs who'd spent decades mastering their industries. They saw problems others missed and had solutions that could change everything. The only thing they lacked was a technical team.

The options were terrible. Hire developers? Too expensive and full of unknowns. Find an agency? They build what you ask for, not what you need. Find a technical co-founder? Like finding a needle in a haystack, and even then, chemistry is rare.

We built Awasero to be the answer. A world-class technical team that works for equity, because we believe in your vision as much as you do.

OUR VALUES

What We Believe

01

Domain Expertise Wins

The best tech companies are built by people who deeply understand the problems they're solving. Technical skills can be provided, but domain expertise must be earned.

02

Skin in the Game

We work for equity because alignment matters. When everyone's success depends on the same outcome, decisions get better and commitment runs deeper.

03

Speed with Discipline

Startups need to move fast, but shortcuts create technical debt that kills companies. We ship quickly without sacrificing the foundation.

04

Radical Transparency

No black boxes. Every decision is explained, every line of code is documented, every metric is shared. Trust is built through transparency.

05

Long-term Thinking

We're not building to flip. We partner with founders who want to create lasting companies that matter. Quick exits aren't our focus.

06

Excellence in Craft

Good enough isn't good enough. We hire senior engineers who take pride in their work and hold ourselves to the highest technical standards.
