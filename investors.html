THE OPPORTUNITY

A Different Kind of Deal Flow

Traditional angel investing means betting on teams you barely know, hoping they can execute on their vision. You're trusting them to hire the right people, build the right product, and navigate the countless technical decisions that determine success.

With Awasero, you invest in companies where we're the technical backbone. We've already validated the founder, assessed the opportunity, and committed our own resources. Our equity stake means our success is directly tied to yours.

This isn't just deal flow. It's pre-vetted opportunities with built-in technical due diligence and aligned incentives from the start.

THE MODEL

How Co-Investment Works

We've designed a model that reduces risk while maintaining high upside potential.

Pre-Vetted Founders

Every founder we partner with has passed our rigorous evaluation process. We assess domain expertise, market opportunity, commitment level, and collaboration fit before committing our resources.

Technical Execution

The biggest risk in early-stage investing is execution. With Awasero as the technical partner, you know the product will be built right. We bring senior engineering talent and proven development practices.

Aligned Incentives

We work for equity, not fees. Our success is your success. We don't get paid unless the company succeeds, which means we're as motivated as anyone to make each venture work.

Ongoing Support

We don't build and leave. As technical co-founders, we stay engaged through growth stages, helping scale the team, architecture, and product as the company evolves.

INVESTMENT STAGES

When to Invest

We facilitate investment at multiple stages, depending on your risk appetite and desired check size.

01

Formation

HIGHEST RISK / HIGHEST RETURN

Invest alongside our initial equity commitment. The company is just forming, the idea is validated but the product is not yet built. Maximum upside potential for those comfortable with early-stage risk.

  • Typical check: $25K - $100K
  • Stage: Idea validation complete
  • Product: Not yet built
02

MVP Complete

MODERATE RISK

Invest once we've built and launched the MVP. You can see the product, evaluate initial user feedback, and assess technical execution before committing capital.

  • Typical check: $50K - $250K
  • Stage: Product launched
  • Metrics: Early user feedback available
03

Seed Extension

LOWER RISK / PROVEN TRACTION

Participate in seed rounds once the company has demonstrated product-market fit. These opportunities have real metrics and proven demand, with Awasero continuing as technical leadership.

  • Typical check: $100K - $500K
  • Stage: Product-market fit demonstrated
  • Metrics: Revenue or strong engagement data
