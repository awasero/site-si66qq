THE CHALLENGE

The Technical Founder Gap

Most transformative ideas come from people who deeply understand an industry's problems. But building a tech company requires technical leadership. This creates an impossible choice: spend months searching for a technical co-founder, pay hundreds of thousands to agencies, or watch your idea remain just an idea.

Traditional options don't work. Hiring developers is expensive and you don't know what you don't know. Agencies build what you ask for, not what you need. Finding a technical co-founder is like finding a needle in a haystack.

Awasero exists to solve this problem. We become your technical co-founder, working for equity because we believe in your vision as much as you do.
