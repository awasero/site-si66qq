FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about partnering with Awasero.

What is a venture software house?

A venture software house provides software development services in exchange for equity rather than traditional fees. Awasero acts as your technical co-founder, building your product and sharing in your company's success.

How do software developers for equity work?

Software developers for equity invest their time and expertise in building your product in exchange for ownership stake. This aligns interests and removes upfront capital requirements.

What do I need to partner with Awasero?

Deep domain expertise in your industry, a validated problem worth solving, commitment to building a company full-time, and openness to a partnership model with shared equity.

How much equity does Awasero take?

Equity arrangements vary based on scope, stage, and founder contributions. Typical arrangements range from 10-30% depending on technical involvement required.

How long does it take to build an MVP?

MVP development typically takes 8-16 weeks. Our agile approach delivers working software in 2-week sprints, allowing you to validate and iterate quickly.

Can Awasero help with AI and machine learning?

Yes. Our team includes specialists in machine learning, NLP, computer vision, and predictive analytics. We help non-technical founders build AI-powered products across industries.
