About

The Awasero Way

We believe the most impactful companies are built at the intersection of deep domain expertise and exceptional technical execution.

Our Mission

To democratize tech entrepreneurship by removing the technical barriers that prevent domain experts from building transformative companies. We partner exclusively with non-technical founders who have deep industry knowledge and a clear vision for solving real problems.

Our Approach

We don't just build software — we build companies. Our equity-based model ensures we're fully invested in your success, with skin in the game from day one.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about partnering with Awasero.

What is a venture software house?

A venture software house is a company that provides software development services in exchange for equity rather than traditional fees. Awasero acts as your technical co-founder, building your product and sharing in your company's success.

How do software developers for equity work?

Software developers for equity invest their time and expertise in building your product in exchange for ownership stake in your company. This aligns interests and removes upfront capital requirements for founders.

What do I need to partner with Awasero?

Non-technical founders need: deep domain expertise in their industry, a validated problem worth solving, commitment to building a company full-time, and openness to a partnership model with shared equity.

How much equity does Awasero take?

Equity arrangements vary based on the scope of work, stage of the company, and founder contributions. Typical arrangements range from 10-30% depending on the level of technical involvement required.

Where is Awasero based?

Awasero is headquartered in Caracas, Venezuela, with a distributed team working globally. We partner with founders from anywhere in the world.

How can I find a technical co-founder for my startup?

Instead of searching for an individual technical co-founder, you can partner with Awasero. We provide an entire technical team that acts as your technical co-founder, bringing experienced engineers, designers, and architects who work for equity in your company.

How long does it take to build an MVP?

MVP development typically takes 8-16 weeks depending on complexity. Our agile approach delivers working software in 2-week sprints, allowing you to validate assumptions and iterate quickly. We prioritize speed-to-market while building a foundation that can scale.

Can Awasero help with AI and machine learning?

Yes, Awasero has extensive experience in AI/ML development. Our team includes specialists in machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and predictive analytics. We help non-technical founders build AI-powered products across industries.

What industries does Awasero work with?

Awasero partners with founders across diverse industries including healthcare, fintech, education, logistics, legal tech, real estate, agriculture, and manufacturing. We look for founders with deep domain expertise who are solving meaningful problems.

How do I apply to partner with Awasero?

To apply for partnership, email partners@awasero.com with information about your background, industry expertise, the problem you're solving, and your vision for the company. Our team reviews applications and schedules discovery calls with founders who are a good fit.
